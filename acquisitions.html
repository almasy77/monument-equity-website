— Acquisitions

Metro Atlanta multifamily buy box.

Monument Equity is acquiring small-to-mid value-add multifamily in metro Atlanta. The active sleeve is sourcing today, with a 2026–2027 goal of 2–3 closings and $1.5–3M of capital deployed. The buy box below is the filter — if a deal sits inside it, we want to see it.

— Buy Box

What we're buying.

Structural / foundation issues, active environmental, occupancy below 60%, owner-occupied, condo conversions, and properties needing rezoning for current use.

— How We Work

A true broker collaboration.

Not a buyer's-agent feed. We'll be doing the hunt with you — sourcing, underwriting, traveling, and bringing deals as much as you bring them to us.

Two-way deal flow

We'll send Crexi pulls, off-market signals, and properties from elsewhere. We want to be the first call on anything in the buy box, or anything you think might interest us.

We underwrite, you partner

Modeling and diligence sit on our side — you don't need to build pro formas. We rely on you for local read: pricing, comps, owner context, and what's quietly being shopped before market.

Speed when it matters

LOI inside 48 hours on the right deal. Lender pre-qual, entity stack, and vendor bench in motion. Atlanta on a moment's notice.

Ground game

10–15 multi-day Atlanta trips per year, walking buildings personally. Kickoff trip is May 18–21, 2026. Identify by late summer 2026; LOI candidate by Labor Day.
